Has our job always been to make ourselves redundant?

Wed, 25 Feb 2026

As I rely more and more on AI to write code, I'm falling into regular periods of existential crisis. What is my purpose as an engineer? Will I still be able to understand code if I stop actually writing it? What concepts do I actually need to commit to memory? Is syntax now irrelevant?
